Notation: a letter is a 90° clockwise turn of that face as you look straight at it. U Up, D Down, R Right, L Left, F Front, B Back. A ' means counter-clockwise, a 2 means a half turn (180°). The centre-fix steps also use slice turns: M the middle layer (between L and R, turned like L), E the equator (between U and D, like D), S the standing layer (between F and B, like F).
